About Us

This is an E-Library for BTech. Computer Science and Engineering Students


This Website will be helpful for CSE students of all semesters. This will help in saving time and efforts while finding a book for a particular semester. Our Website provides links to buy and download books suggested by IK Gujral Punjab Technical University. Students can chosose their semester from the dropdown provided on Home Page. Links to all the Subject Books are provided along with their Title and Author's name. The chosen book can then be downloaded or bought according to the link provided.
